Quick Setup

1

Download the Bridge

Download MSFS-Bridge.exe from the releases page. This connects Microsoft Flight Simulator to your browser.

2

Start Flight Simulator

Launch MSFS 2020 or 2024 and load into a flight. The sim must be running before starting the bridge.

3

Run the Bridge

Double-click MSFS-Bridge.exe. You'll see "SimConnect connected" when it's working.

4

Open Maneuver Tracker

Click any maneuver above. The status should show "Connected" and you'll see live data from your sim!
